What is booklist?

Booklist is a publication of the American Library Association that provides critical reviews of books and audiovisual materials for all ages. It is geared toward libraries and booksellers and is available in print or online. It is published twice monthly September through June and monthly in July and August. Read More on Wikipedia

What is a good book to me?

A good book is the one which makes an impact on me, makes me think about the theme that it talks about. Makes me curious and restless to know what will happen next ( in case its a fiction), the protagonist stays in your mind and you ponder upon his/her actions,decisions and dialogues. As curious you are to finish it, you may sense of fulfillment and a longing to read more when you read the last word. Sometimes, very rarely, you find yourself shedding a tear or two when you read the last word and when you lift your head up you realize the world around you hasn't changed, but you have.

What really knocks me out is a book that, when you're all done reading it, you wish the author that wrote it was a terrific friend of yours and you could call him up on the phone whenever you felt like it. That doesn't happen much, though.

--J. D. Salinger, The Catcher in the Rye

In short, a good book is one that leaves you thinking, wondering, questioning, smiling, laughing, crying, shivering in fear. A good book is one that leaves you shrouded in mystery, or feeling content, or longing nostalgically for a time and place out of your reach. A good book leaves you both happy for the experience and sad for the completion of the journey embarked hours ago.?
